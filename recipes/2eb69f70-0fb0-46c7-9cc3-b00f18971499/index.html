Mushroom and Olive Pizza

A delicious homemade pizza featuring earthy mushrooms, salty olives, and a generous layer of melted cheese, all on a crispy golden crust. Perfect for a casual dinner or a get-together with friends.

Prep Time

20 min

Cook Time

15 min

Total Time

35 min

Servings

4

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •1.0 ball Pizza dough
  • •0.5 cup Pizza sauce
  • •1.0 cup Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • •0.5 cup Cremini mushrooms, sliced
  • •0.2 cup Black olives, sliced
  • •1.0 tablespoon Olive oil
  • •0.2 teaspoon Dried oregano
  • •0.1 teaspoon Garlic powder
  • •1.0 pinch Salt
  • •1.0 pinch Black pepper

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories450

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

  2. 2

    On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape (usually 12-14 inches round).

  3. 3

    Carefully transfer the dough to a pizza peel lightly dusted with cornmeal or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  4. 4

    Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

  5. 5

    Sprinkle half of the mozzarella cheese over the sauce.

  6. 6

    Arrange the sliced mushrooms and olives over the cheese.

  7. 7

    Top with the remaining mozzarella cheese, then sprinkle with dried oregano and garlic powder.

  8. 8

    Drizzle lightly with olive oil and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.

  9. 9

    Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven or pizza stone.

  10. 10

    Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  11. 11

    Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes before slicing, and serve hot.
