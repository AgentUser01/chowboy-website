Leftover Pizza Revival

This recipe focuses on using a leftover slice of pizza as the base to create a quick, satisfying meal by reheating it and optionally adding fresh toppings if available.

Prep Time

2 min

Cook Time

10 min

Total Time

12 min

Servings

1

Difficulty

Easy

Ingredients

  • •1.0 slice Leftover pizza
  • •0.5 tsp Dried Italian herbs (optional)
  • •0.5 cup Shredded mozzarella (optional)

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories350

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat your oven or toaster oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. 2

    Place the leftover pizza slice directly on the oven rack or on a baking sheet.

  3. 3

    If desired, sprinkle with extra shredded mozzarella and dried Italian herbs.

  4. 4

    Bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is crispy.

  5. 5

    Carefully remove from the oven and let cool for a minute before serving.
