Parsley Walnut Pesto

A vibrant and nutty pesto made with fresh parsley and crunchy walnuts, perfect for tossing with pasta, spreading on sandwiches, or topping grilled chicken and fish.

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

0 min

Total Time

10 min

Servings

4

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •2.0 cups fresh parsley, packed
  • •0.5 cup walnuts
  • •2.0 cloves garlic
  • •0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • •0.5 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • •0.5 tsp salt
  • •0.2 tsp black pepper

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories300

Instructions

  1. 1

    Wash and thoroughly dry the fresh parsley. Remove any thick stems.

  2. 2

    Place the walnuts and peeled garlic cloves into a food processor. Pulse a few times until roughly chopped.

  3. 3

    Add the fresh parsley to the food processor. Pulse until finely chopped, scraping down the sides as needed.

  4. 4

    Add the grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper to the mixture. Pulse again to combine.

  5. 5

    With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the extra virgin olive oil until the pesto reaches your desired consistency. It should be thick but pourable.

  6. 6

    Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. You may add more salt, pepper, or cheese to your liking.
