Hearty Breakfast Scramble

A quick and satisfying breakfast scramble packed with eggs, crispy bacon, savory mushrooms, and vibrant bell peppers. Perfect for a weekend brunch or a substantial weekday meal.

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

15 min

Total Time

25 min

Servings

2

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •4.0 large eggs
  • •1.0 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • •1.0 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • •2.0 tablespoons milk or cream (optional)
  • •0.5 teaspoon salt
  • •0.2 teaspoon black pepper
  • •1.0 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories450

Instructions

  1. 1

    Sauté vegetables: Add diced bell pepper and sliced mushrooms to the skillet with olive oil or butter. Cook over medium heat for 5-7 minutes, until vegetables are tender-crisp. Remove vegetables from the skillet and set aside.

  2. 2

    Scramble the eggs: In a medium bowl, whisk the eggs with milk or cream (if using), salt, and pepper until well combined. Pour the egg mixture into the same skillet over medium-low heat.

  3. 3

    Finish the scramble: As the eggs begin to set, gently push them from the edges towards the center with a spatula. When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly moist, return the cooked vegetables to the skillet. Gently fold everything together until heated through.

  4. 4

    Serve immediately.
