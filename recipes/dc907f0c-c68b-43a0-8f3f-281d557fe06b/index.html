Marmasetti

A classic Marmasetti recipe featuring ground beef, noodles, and a rich tomato sauce, baked to perfection.

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

60 min

Total Time

75 min

Servings

6

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •4.0 onions, chopped
  • •1.0 tbsp fat
  • •2.0 lb ground beef
  • •1.0 tin tomato soup, condensed
  • •1.0 medium tin tomato juice
  • •3.0 tbsp sugar
  • •1.0 tsp paprika
  • •1.0 to taste salt
  • •1.0 to taste pepper
  • •12.0 oz medium noodles, cooked
  • •8.0 oz grated cheese

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories650

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).

  2. 2

    In a large bowl, combine the chopped onions, fat, ground beef, condensed tomato soup, medium tin of tomato juice, sugar, paprika, salt, and pepper.

  3. 3

    Mix well, ensuring the ground beef is thoroughly combined but do not brown it.

  4. 4

    Add the cooked medium noodles to the mixture and stir until everything is evenly distributed.

  5. 5

    Fold in the grated cheese.

  6. 6

    Transfer the mixture to a baking dish.

  7. 7

    Bake uncovered for 1 hour or 45 minutes at 350°F (175°C), or until bubbly and heated through.
