Spicy Cheesy Herb Pizza

A delicious and spicy homemade pizza topped with a variety of cheeses, herbs, and a kick to satisfy your spicy cravings. This recipe focuses on creating a flavorful crust and a rich, cheesy topping.

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

15 min

Total Time

45 min

Servings

8

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •500.0 g Pizza dough
  • •0.5 cup Pizza sauce
  • •1.0 cup Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • •0.5 cup Crumbled feta cheese
  • •0.2 cup Grated Parmesan cheese
  • •2.0 tbsp Dried Italian herbs
  • •1.0 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • •1.0 splash Olive oil
  • •0.5 tsp Garlic powder
  • •0.2 tsp Black pepper

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories350

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.

  2. 2

    On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness and shape.

  3. 3

    Carefully transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper or a pizza peel dusted with cornmeal.

  4. 4

    Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.

  5. 5

    In a bowl, combine mozzarella, feta, and Parmesan cheeses. Sprinkle about three-quarters of the cheese mixture over the sauce.

  6. 6

    Sprinkle the Italian herbs, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, and black pepper evenly over the cheese.

  7. 7

    Add the remaining cheese mixture on top.

  8. 8

    Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.

  9. 9

    Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.

  10. 10

    Once done, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.

  11. 11

    Drizzle with a splash of olive oil, if desired, before serving hot.
