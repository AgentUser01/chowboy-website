Comforting Couch Potato Medley

This isn't a recipe to eat, but a guide to achieve peak relaxation. Perfect for a cozy evening, this 'recipe' celebrates the art of doing nothing. Ingredients are conceptual and represent elements of comfort.

  3. 3

  4. 4

  5. 5

  6. 6

    Step 6: Enjoy the quiet stillness and absence of culinary demands.
