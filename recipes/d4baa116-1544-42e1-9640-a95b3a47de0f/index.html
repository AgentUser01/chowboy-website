Crispy Baked Chicken Thighs

Simple and delicious bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs baked to crispy perfection with a savory spice blend.

⏱️
Prep Time

10 min

🔥
Cook Time

40 min

⏰
Total Time

50 min

🍽️
Servings

4

📊
Difficulty

Medium

🥘 Ingredients

  • 4.0 pieces bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1.0 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1.0 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon onion powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon paprika

📊 Nutrition (per serving)

Calories350

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).

  2. 2

    Pat the chicken thighs thoroughly dry with paper towels. This is crucial for crispy skin.

  3. 3

    In a small bowl, combine salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and paprika.

  4. 4

    Place the chicken thighs on a clean surface. Drizzle them with olive oil, then rub the spice mixture evenly over all sides of the chicken, making sure to get some under the skin if desired.

  5. 5

    Arrange the chicken thighs skin-side up on a baking sheet or in a shallow roasting pan. Ensure there is some space between each piece for even cooking and browning.

  6. 6

    Bake for 35-45 minutes, or until the internal temperature of the thickest part of the thigh reaches 175°F (80°C) and the skin is golden brown and crispy. If the skin isn't crispy enough, you can broil for the last 2-3 minutes, watching carefully to prevent burning.

  7. 7

    Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for 5-10 minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute, keeping the chicken moist.
