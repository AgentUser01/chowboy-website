Campfire Paella

  • ‚Ä¢Preparation Time: 20 minutes
  • ‚Ä¢Cooking Time: 40 minutes
  • ‚Ä¢Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • ‚Ä¢1 cup paella rice
  • ‚Ä¢2 cups chicken stock (pre-measured and stored in a leak-proof container)
  • ‚Ä¢1 small onion, diced
  • ‚Ä¢2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ‚Ä¢1/2 lb chicken thigh, cut into pieces
  • ‚Ä¢1/2 lb shrimp, peeled
  • ‚Ä¢1/2 cup green peas
  • ‚Ä¢1 bell pepper, sliced
  • ‚Ä¢2 tomatoes, diced
  • ‚Ä¢1 teaspoon paprika
  • ‚Ä¢Salt and pepper to taste
  • ‚Ä¢Olive oil
  • ‚Ä¢Lemon wedges, for serving

Instructions:

  1. 1
    Prepare the Fire: Get your campfire to a steady medium heat.
  2. 2
    Cook the Chicken: In a large skillet or paella pan, heat olive oil over the campfire. Add chicken pieces, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. 3
    Saut√© Vegetables: Add onions, garlic, and bell peppers to the pan. Cook until the onions are translucent.
  4. 4
    Combine Ingredients: Return the chicken to the pan, add rice, tomatoes, paprika, and slowly pour in the chicken stock. Stir everything together.
  5. 5
    Let it Simmer: Reduce the heat (move the pan to a cooler side of the campfire) and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the rice is almost cooked.
  6. 6
    Add the Shrimp and Peas: Place the shrimp and peas on top, cover with foil, and cook for an additional 10 minutes.
  7. 7
    Serve with Lemon Wedges: Check that the rice and shrimp are cooked through. Serve your campfire paella with a squeeze of fresh lemon.
