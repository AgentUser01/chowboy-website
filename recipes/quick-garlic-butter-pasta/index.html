Quick Garlic Butter Pasta

A simple, delicious pasta dish that comes together in just 15 minutes. Perfect for busy weeknights when you want something comforting and flavorful.

⏱️
Prep Time

5 min

🔥
Cook Time

10 min

⏰
Total Time

15 min

🍽️
Servings

4

📊
Difficulty

Easy

quick mealspastavegetarianweeknight dinner

🥘 Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti or linguine
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • to taste salt and black pepper
  • 2 tbsp pasta water (reserved)

📊 Nutrition (per serving)

Calories485
Protein15g
Carbs68g
Fat17g

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. 1

    Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook pasta according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water before draining.

  2. 2

    While pasta cooks, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.

  3. 3

    Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the garlic butter. Toss to coat, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time to create a silky sauce.

  4. 4

    Remove from heat and stir in fresh parsley and half the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

  5. 5

    Serve immediately, topped with remaining Parmesan cheese and extra parsley if desired.
