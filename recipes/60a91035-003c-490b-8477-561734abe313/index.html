Thanksgiving Leftover Casserole

"Turkey, mashed potatoes, cheese, a special sauce, and some little extras combine to make a tasty casserole for leftover turkey. An absolutely delicious way to use those tasty Thanksgiving leftovers." Original recipe posted at allrecipes.

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

45 min

Total Time

75 min

Servings

8

Difficulty

Medium

Ingredients

  • •3.0 tablespoons butter
  • •2.0 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • •0.5 cup milk
  • •0.8 cup water
  • •0.2 teaspoon salt
  • •0.2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • •0.1 teaspoon onion powder
  • •2.0 tablespoons cheddar cheese
  • •1.0 cup diced cooked turkey meat
  • •1.0 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • •2.0 cups leftover mashed potatoes

Nutrition (per serving)

Calories366

Instructions

  1. 1

    Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  2. 2

    Melt 3 tablespoons butter in a saucepan over low heat. Blend in the flour. Slowly stir in 1/2 cup milk and 3/4 cup water, then season with salt, pepper, and onion powder. Stir sauce over low heat for 5 minutes.

  3. 3

    In a separate saucepan over low heat, melt 2 tablespoons butter. Stir in the dry stuffing mix. Place the turkey in the prepared baking dish. Pour the sauce over turkey. First sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Spread mashed potatoes over cheese. Top mashed potatoes with the stuffing mixture.

  4. 4

    Bake 45 minutes in the preheated oven.
